Controls

  • Press W or ↑ to move forward
  • Press A or ← to move left
  • Press D or → to move right
  • Press T for a sip of Twinings Earl Grey
  • Press S to take a bite of a sausage roll (V for vegan option)
  • Hold I to ignore your need to use the toilet
  • Do not leave the queue for any reason
  • Estimated queue time: 13 hours and 30 minutes
