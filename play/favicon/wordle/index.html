This page is running a version of Wordle... kind of. It's in the favicon!

(Which means it won't work if you're on mobile or Safari, sorry!)

Start typing to enter your guess. The guess gets submitted as soon as you've entered the fifth letter, and you get six guesses.

Green means the right letter in the right place
Yellow means the right letter in the wrong place
Grey means the letter isn't in the word

Refresh the page for a new word!
(And check out the browser console for more details)
