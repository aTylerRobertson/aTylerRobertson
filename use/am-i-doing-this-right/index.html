Am I doing right?

NO.

And no one else is, either.

No one knows the "right" way to do anything.

Anyone who thinks they're doing anything the right way is basing that on ideas that somebody else came up with—and that person had no clue how to do anything when they started, and just made stuff up.

When you're doing anything, the more important question is, "Am I enjoying it?"

Because if the answer is "yes", and you're not hurting anybody, then you're already leading a much more fulfilling life than the people who claim to hold the secret to anything, because you're doing it the right way for you. And that's what actually matters.
